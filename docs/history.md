# 湍河流域水利史 · A Water History of the Tuan River Basin

> 本文是地图数据的史料背景说明，按年代叙述邓州、内乡、新野三县（市）境内湍河及其水系上的水利工程。每节先中文、后 English。括注为主要史料/来源。
>
> Background notes for the map data: a chronological account of water-conservancy works on the Tuan River and its drainage within Dengzhou, Neixiang and Xinye. Each section is given in Chinese then English; parentheses cite the principal sources.

地理锚点 / Geographic anchor：邓州城关 ≈ **32.68°N, 112.08°E**（北纬 32°41′、东经 112°05′）。

---

## 一、春秋战国：楚国与邓国 · Spring & Autumn – Warring States: Chu and the State of Deng

**中文.** 湍河流域最早的水利可追溯到楚国。《水经注·湍水》载**楚堨（楚堰）**：“高下相承八重，周十里，方塘蓄水，泽润不穷”——这是一组**八级跌水的蓄水陂塘**，约在今邓州西北、十林/林扒一带的湍河上游，灌田五百余顷（明嘉靖《南阳府志》作二百余顷），元、明续修，是西汉六门陂在工程理念上的先声。与水利相邻的是**楚长城（方城）**：其“方城以为城，汉水以为池”的方略，把湍河等河流当作天然护城水系；长城自内乡入邓州、跨湍河南下，邓州境内尚存约 30 km。邓国为春秋小国，公元前 678 年为楚文王所灭（《左传》庄公十六年）；其早期“城濠”是人工壕沟与天然河道的结合，而非工程化运河。

**English.** The earliest waterworks here belong to the state of Chu. The *Commentary on the Water Classic* records the **Chu Weir (楚堨/楚堰)** as "eight stepped impoundments, ten li around, square ponds storing water without end" — a cascade of eight terraced ponds on the upper Tuan north-west of today's Dengzhou (Shilin/Linba area), watering 500+ qing (a Ming gazetteer says 200+). It is the conceptual forerunner of the Han Six-Gate system. Alongside it ran the **Chu Great Wall (Fangcheng)**, whose doctrine "walls of Fangcheng, moats of the Han River" turned the Tuan and other rivers into natural moats; a ~30 km segment survives in Dengzhou. The small state of Deng was annexed by King Wen of Chu in 678 BCE (*Zuo Zhuan*); its early "moat" was a dug ditch combined with a natural watercourse rather than an engineered canal.

## 二、两汉：召信臣、杜诗与六门陂 · The Han: Shao Xinchen, Du Shi and the Six-Gate Reservoir

**中文.** 西汉元帝时，南阳太守**召信臣**于**建昭五年（公元前 34 年）断湍水，立穰西石堨**，初开三石门；**元始五年（公元 5 年）**扩为**六石门**，故名“六门”（《水经注·湍水》）。坝体横跨湍河成溢流堰，下结二十余陂，构成“长藤结瓜”式灌溉网，**溉穰、新野、涅阳（一作昆阳/朝阳）三县五千余顷**。同时他在穰县东南六十里筑**钳卢陂**，“累石为堤，旁开六石门以节水势”，沟通刁河（朝水）与湍河两个水系（《元和郡县志》《通典》）。召信臣“行视郡中水泉……起水门提阏凡数十处”，并刻石立**均水约束**，《汉书·循吏传》记其灌溉“多至三万顷”。东汉**杜诗**任南阳太守（建武七年，公元 31 年），发明**水排**（水力鼓风冶铁），又“修治陂池，广拓土田”。南阳由是有“**前有召父，后有杜母**”之谣——这正是“**父母官**”一词的由来。

**English.** Under Emperor Yuan of the Western Han, the Nanyang governor **Shao Xinchen** **dammed the Tuan west of Rang with a stone weir in 34 BCE**, opening three stone gates; in **AD 5** these were enlarged to **six stone gates** — hence "Six-Gate" (*Commentary on the Water Classic*). The overflow weir fed a chain of ~20+ ponds in a "vine-and-melons" network that **irrigated 5,000+ qing across three counties** (Rang, Xinye and Nieyang/Kunyang). Sixty li south-east he raised **Qianlu Reservoir** — "a dam of piled stone with six stone gates to regulate the flow" — linking the Diao and Tuan basins (*Yuanhe Gazetteers*; *Tongdian*). Shao also inscribed China's earliest recorded **water-allocation rules** in stone; the *Book of Han* credits his Nanyang works with up to "30,000 qing". In the Eastern Han, governor **Du Shi** (from AD 31) invented the **water-powered blast-furnace bellows** and "repaired the ponds and extended the fields". Nanyang's saying "**first Father Shao, then Mother Du**" is the origin of the Chinese term **fumǔguān** ("parent-official").

## 三、魏晋至明清：屡修屡废 · From the Jin to the Qing: repair and decline

**中文.** 汉末建安十三年（208 年）赤壁之役前后，曹操军曾在穰城南郊开**运粮河**（军用运渠）。西晋太康三年（**282 年**），镇南大将军**杜预**大修，“复六门陂……下结二十九陂”，延干渠贯邓县—新野百余里入白河——这次著名的复修是**杜预**所为，史籍常误作东汉**杜诗**，二者实为两人两事。北宋真宗时知州谢绛曾在召信臣旧基上部分复修。明洪武初知州孔显疏浚；**嘉靖三十三年（1554 年）知州王道升重修，增至三十八陂、十四堰**；约明末崇祯以后失修淤废，前后行用约 1600 年。今岔股路村北存渠首、韩凹村存渡槽残段，为邓州文物保护单位。汉代见于《水经注》的还有**邓氏陂、安众港**（湍水）、**樊氏陂**（朝水/刁河）、**新野陂、豫章大陂**（淯水/白河，新野一带）等陂渠。

**English.** Around the Red Cliffs campaign of AD 208, Cao Cao's army dug a **grain-transport canal** south of Rang. In **AD 282** the Jin general **Du Yu** carried out the great restoration — "he restored the Six-Gate Reservoir … and linked twenty-nine ponds below it" — extending the trunk canal over 100 li across Deng and Xinye into the Bai River. (This famous rebuild was **Du Yu**, often miswritten as the Eastern-Han **Du Shi** — two different men.) The Northern-Song prefect Xie Jiang partly rebuilt it on Shao's old foundations; in the Ming, prefect Kong Xian dredged it (early Hongwu) and **in 1554 prefect Wang Daosheng rebuilt it to 38 ponds and 14 weirs**; after the late Ming it silted up and was abandoned, having served ~1,600 years. The canal head north of Chagulu village and a trough-canal section near Hanwa survive as a protected relic of Dengzhou. Other Han-era ponds named in the *Commentary* include **Dengshi Reservoir** and **Anzhong canal** (on the Tuan), **Fanshi Reservoir** (on the Diao), and **Xinye Reservoir** and **Yuzhang Great Reservoir** (on the Bai, around Xinye).

## 四、民国至今：从湍惠渠到南水北调 · Republic to present: from the Tuanhui Canal to the S-to-N Transfer

**中文.** 近代湍河引水以**湍惠渠**为代表：渠首位于罗庄镇西一道约 550 m 拦河堰左岸，1942 年初建，1957—1984 年间四次重修，自流灌溉约 2500 公顷，并补给半坡水库。1949 年后，三县大兴水利：内乡建**斩龙岗（1969）、打磨岗（1981，中型）、云露湖（2014）**等水库并“库库连通”供打磨岗灌区；邓州现有水库 17 座（含中型刘山、张沟），多分布在西部丘陵的湍河支流上；新野地处下游平原，少有水库，主要靠引丹与提灌。流域现代灌溉的骨干是 **1974 年通水的引丹灌区**——自丹江口水库经**陶岔渠首**北引，灌溉邓州、新野。2014 年，**南水北调中线**总干渠经邓州西部北送，并以**湍河渡槽**（世界最大 U 形渡槽，全长 1030 m，三槽并联、设计流量 350 m³/s）凌空跨越湍河。城区段则建有**邓州、内乡橡胶坝群**抬水成景，并设**湍河国家湿地公园**（约 1699.7 公顷，2014 年获批国家级）。

**English.** The flagship modern diversion is the **Tuanhui Canal**: its head sluice sits on a ~550 m cross-river weir west of Luozhuang Town, first built in 1942 and rebuilt four times (1957–1984), gravity-irrigating ~2,500 ha. After 1949 the three counties built extensively: Neixiang raised **Zhanlonggang (1969), Damogang (1981, medium) and Yunluhu (2014)** reservoirs, interconnected to feed the Damogang district; Dengzhou now has 17 reservoirs (including the medium Liushan and Zhanggou), mostly on Tuan tributaries in the western hills; Xinye, on the downstream plain, has few reservoirs and relies on canal and pumped irrigation. The backbone of modern irrigation is the **Yindan district, opened in 1974**, drawing from Danjiangkou Reservoir through the **Taocha headworks** to water Dengzhou and Xinye. In 2014 the **South-to-North Water Transfer (Middle Route)** main canal began running north through western Dengzhou, crossing the Tuan on the **Tuanhe Aqueduct** — the world's largest U-shaped aqueduct (1,030 m, three parallel troughs, design flow 350 m³/s). In the towns, **rubber-dam groups** in Dengzhou and Neixiang pond the river into urban water surfaces, and the **Tuanhe National Wetland Park** (~1,699.7 ha, national-grade since 2014) protects the city reach.

## 五、围堰：要素，也是营造技术 · Cofferdams: feature and technique

**中文.** 湍河流域没有作为独立纪念物留存的“古代围堰”，但“**围堰/围堤**”自古就是**筑陂蓄水的关键工法**：六门陂、钳卢陂乃至汝南鸿隙陂，都是“四周修筑围堤”壅水成库（张芳《中国古代灌溉工程技术史》）。近现代工程中，围堰多为施工临时构筑，如**陶岔渠首改建**期间以约 2000 m³ 临时混凝土形成的**纵向围堰**，以维持过流、保护基坑。本系统因此把围堰同时作为可点选的“工程要素”和贯穿各陂塘的“营造技术”来呈现。

**English.** No standalone *ancient* cofferdam survives in the basin, but the **cofferdam / coffer-dike** has always been the key *method* of impoundment: the Six-Gate and Qianlu reservoirs — like Hongxi Reservoir in Runan — were all raised by "encircling embankments" (Zhang Fang, *A Technical History of Ancient Chinese Irrigation*). In modern works cofferdams are temporary: during the **Taocha headworks** reconstruction a **longitudinal cofferdam** of ~2,000 m³ of concrete maintained flow and protected the excavation. The GIS therefore treats the cofferdam both as a selectable *feature* and as the *construction technique* running through every pond and reservoir.

## 存疑 · Uncertainties

1. **建坝年代 / Founding year** — 史载六门堰始建为“建昭五年=前 34 年”；前 38 年只是建昭元年。本系统取 **−34**。 / All sources give 34 BCE; 38 BCE is merely the start of the reign-era. We use **−34**.
2. **第三受益县 / Third beneficiary county** — 《水经注》作“昆阳”，张芳校为“涅阳”，科技日报作“朝阳”；穰、新野两县无异。 / "Kunyang" vs "Nieyang" vs "Chaoyang"; the first two counties are consistent.
3. **282 年大修者 / The AD 282 restorer** — 为西晋**杜预**，常误作东汉**杜诗**。 / It was the Jin general **Du Yu**, often confused with the Han governor **Du Shi**.
4. **“三十六陂”** — 此系统无据；可考为晋“二十九陂”、明“三十八陂十四堰”。 / Not attested for this system; the documented counts are 29 ponds (Jin) and 38 ponds + 14 weirs (Ming).
5. **灌溉顷数 / Irrigated acreage** — 三万/四万/万/五千余顷诸说混杂（单工程 vs 全系统 vs 方志夸饰），均按量级看待。 / The various qing figures mix per-feature, whole-system and gazetteer hyperbole; treat as orders of magnitude.
6. **历史坐标 / Historical coordinates** — 史料无经纬度，均由城关锚点 + 方位里程推定，`confidence` 标注把握度，宜实地核校。 / No source gives coordinates; all are inferred from the city anchor plus bearings, with confidence stated, and should be field-verified.
